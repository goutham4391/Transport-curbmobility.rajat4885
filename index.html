Reimagining urban mobility and building the transportation network of tomorrow.

Reimagining urban mobility. What does it mean to reimagine urban mobility? It starts with our platform – a strong foundation that connects every aspect of the for-hire ride experience. The Curb Mobility platform provides a home for our mobile app, payment, paratransit, business travel, and advertising solutions that touch millions of riders and drivers nationwide. In short, We are creating the foundation for a scalable, efficient, intelligent, open transportation network of the future.

Curb Business provides a centralized tool for efficient employee travel management. The flexible platform allows you to set travel rules and budgets, and allows your staff to easily book trips with an app. Plus, there’s no need to ever worry about expensive surge pricing.

Curb Concierge is a platform that lets you book taxis for guests from any computer, tablet, smartphone or from a countertop kiosk.
